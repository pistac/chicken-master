Instructions

  • You will watch several clips where an animated character is describing an object.
  • Please rate each clip in terms of arousal and valence.
  • Arousal refers to whether the character is displaying high or low activation, such as excitedness or calmness.
  • Valence refers to whether the character is displaying a positive or negative emotion, such as happiness or sadness.
  • To rate a clip, click on the images corresponding to how the agent in the clip is feeling.
  • After rating a clip, click "next" to move on to the next clip.
  • Some clips will have audio and video, some only audio, some only video. Please watch and listen to each clip carefully.
  • Please wear headphones and adjust the volume to a comfortable level.

Consent

  • By clicking "Next" below, you agree to take part in this study.
  • Data from this study will be be kept confidential and only shown in aggregate form.
  • This study is conducted in accordance with the ethical guidelines of KTH Royal Institute of Technology.

Browser compatibility

For best results, use Chrome or Firefox. You won't be able to complete the task if you are using Internet Explorer.

Please note that you must wear headphones to take part.

Let's get acquainted with the task.

First, you will see this image. Clicking on it will play a video of an animated character:

The clips will sometimes have audio, sometimes not. Sometimes they will have no video, like this one:

After the clip stops playing, scroll down to see the following rating scales. Click on the image that best represents how the character in the clip is feeling, in terms of arousal and valence. The rating that you select is highlighted in grey:

Remember, arousal refers to how excited or calm the animated character is; valence refers to whether it's displaying a positive or negative emotion. Try to focus on how the character in the clips is feeling, rather than what it is saying.

You can watch the clip as many times as you need, by clicking on it.

Once you are happy with the rating, click "Next" to proceed to the next clip.

Important! You will need to provide one rating for arousal and one for valence before moving to the next clip.
